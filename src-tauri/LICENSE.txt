DECISION SUPPORT, NOT INVESTMENT ADVICE.

Private Terminal is a personal research and visualization tool. The
indicators, charts, and analytics shown are descriptive — they describe
historical data and current market state. They are not predictions, signals,
or recommendations. Decisions about your money are yours alone, made under
your own judgment and at your own risk.

This applies to all charts, indicators, percentile rankings, regime
classifications, and any other output of the Software, including but not
limited to: SMMA Ribbon state, RSI, ATR, Drawdown, Volume Profile, Anchored
VWAP, Pulse cross-section heatmap, and all Analysis-section tools.

DATA SOURCES.

Private Terminal queries the following public data sources to populate its
displays. Each is operated by a third party under their own terms; the
Software is not affiliated with any of them.

  - FRED (Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis) — macroeconomic series
  - Yahoo Finance (unofficial endpoint) — quotes and historical bars
  - Finnhub — US-equity news (only when an API key is supplied)
  - News RSS feeds — public broadcast feeds you choose to enable

No outbound calls leave the machine other than these legitimate requests
to the public data sources, and external links you click that open in your
default browser. The full list is documented inside the application at
Settings → Privacy.
